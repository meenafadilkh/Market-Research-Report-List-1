<p><strong>The Semiconductor Equipment Air Bearing Spindle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aerostatic Bearing Spindle</li><li>Aerodynamic Bearing Spindle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor Equipment Air Bearing Spindle market comprises two primary types: aerostatic and aerodynamic bearing spindles. Aerostatic bearing spindles utilize a thin film of pressurized air to support the spindle, providing precise motion and minimal friction. In contrast, aerodynamic bearing spindles rely on the high-speed rotation of the spindle to create air pressure, achieving stability and low vibration levels. Both types are essential for enhancing the performance and efficiency of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
