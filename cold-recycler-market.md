Asphalt emulsion recyclers utilize a combination of water, asphalt emulsions, and additives to rejuvenate and reinforce existing pavement. In contrast, foamed asphalt recyclers expand asphalt binder with steam, creating a lightweight mixture ideal for base course applications. Both market types aim to reduce waste and improve infrastructure efficiency by repurposing aged materials, catering to the growing demand for environmentally friendly construction solutions.</p></p>
