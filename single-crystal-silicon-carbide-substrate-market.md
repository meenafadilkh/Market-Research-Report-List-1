<p><p>The Single Crystal Silicon Carbide Substrate market consists of two main types: conductive and semi-insulating substrates. Conductive substrates are often used in high-power and high-frequency applications due to their excellent electrical conductivity, making them ideal for devices like transistors and diodes. Semi-insulating substrates, on the other hand, possess minimal electrical conductivity, which makes them suitable for applications requiring reduced noise and interference, such as RF devices and high-temperature electronics. Both types are essential in advancing semiconductor technology.</p></p>
