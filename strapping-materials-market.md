<p><p>The strapping materials market is primarily categorized into three types: steel, polypropylene, and polyester. Steel strapping is known for its high strength and durability, making it ideal for heavy loads and industrial applications. Polypropylene strapping is lightweight and flexible, suitable for lighter packages and offers resistance to moisture and UV damage. Polyester strapping provides a balance between strength and flexibility, often used in securing heavy packages while resisting elongation and impact, making it popular in various industries.</p></p>
