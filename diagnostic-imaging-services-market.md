<p><p>The Diagnostic Imaging Services market encompasses various imaging modalities used for medical diagnosis. Diagnostic Radiology includes X-rays and fluoroscopy for visualizing structures. Ultrasound employs high-frequency sound waves to produce images, often used in obstetrics. Computed Tomography (CT) uses X-rays and computer processing for cross-sectional imaging. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) utilizes magnetic fields and radio waves for detailed soft tissue images. Nuclear Imaging involves radioactive substances to assess organ function. Other modalities may include emerging technologies and hybrid systems, enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
