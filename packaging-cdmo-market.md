<p><ul><li>Primary Packaging</li><li>Secondary Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Packaging Contract Development and Manufacturing Organization (CDMO) market is divided into two main types: primary packaging and secondary packaging. Primary packaging refers to the materials that directly contain and protect the product, such as bottles, blisters, or pouches, ensuring product integrity and compliance. Secondary packaging encompasses the outer packaging that groups primary packages, providing additional protection and branding, such as boxes or cartons. Both types are crucial for product safety, shelf appeal, and efficient distribution in various industries.</p></p>
