<p><strong>The Retail RFID Security Tags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HF Tags</li><li>UHF Tags</li><li>LF Tags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Retail RFID security tags are categorized into three main types based on their frequency: HF (High Frequency), UHF (Ultra High Frequency), and LF (Low Frequency). HF tags, operating at 13.56 MHz, are often used for electronic article surveillance and payment systems. UHF tags, functioning between 300 MHz and 3 GHz, excel in inventory management and long-range tracking. LF tags, operating below 300 kHz, are typically used for asset tracking and animal identification. Each type serves distinct applications, enhancing security and operational efficiency in retail environments.</p></p>
