<p><strong>The GigE Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera</li><li>Area Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GigE camera market is primarily divided into two types: line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras capture image data one line at a time, making them ideal for high-speed applications like web inspection and surface quality analysis. In contrast, area scan cameras capture an entire image in a single frame, making them suitable for general imaging tasks in industrial automation, medical imaging, and security. Both types leverage GigE technology for high-speed data transfer and effective imaging solutions.</p></p>
