<p><strong>The Emergency Contraceptives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combined estrogen and progestin pills</li><li>Progestin-only (as Levonorgestrel) pills</li><li>Antiprogestin (as mifepristone) pills</li><li>Ulipristal acetate pills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emergency contraceptives market includes various types, each serving to prevent pregnancy after unprotected intercourse. Combined estrogen and progestin pills work by inhibiting ovulation and altering the uterine lining. Progestin-only pills, such as Levonorgestrel, primarily function by delaying ovulation. Antiprogestin pills, like mifepristone, block progesterone to disrupt implantation. Ulipristal acetate pills offer a selective progesterone receptor modulation, effectively delaying ovulation as well. Together, these methods cater to diverse needs and preferences in post-coital contraceptive options.</p></p>
