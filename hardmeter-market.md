<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Hardmeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brinell Hardmeter</li><li>Vickers Hardmeter</li><li>Rockwell Hardmeter</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hardmeter market comprises various types based on measurement principles. Brinell hardness testers utilize a hardened steel or carbide ball to indent the material, assessing hardness via indentation diameter. Vickers hardness testers employ a diamond pyramid indenter, providing a broad measurement range and suited for small materials. Rockwell hardness testers measure the depth of indentation under a specific load, offering quick results. Other types include Knoop and Leeb hardness testers, catering to diverse applications and materials in different industries.</p></p>
