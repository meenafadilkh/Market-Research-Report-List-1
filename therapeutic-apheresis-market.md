<p><strong>The Therapeutic Apheresis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Therapeutic Plasma Exchange</li><li>Red Blood Cell Exchange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The therapeutic apheresis market encompasses various procedures aimed at treating certain medical conditions by selectively removing components from a patient's blood. Therapeutic plasma exchange (TPE) involves removing plasma, which may contain harmful substances, and replacing it with a substitute, helping manage autoimmune disorders and neurological conditions. Red blood cell exchange focuses on removing diseased or damaged red blood cells, particularly in patients with sickle cell disease, to improve oxygen delivery and reduce complications. Both procedures play crucial roles in patient care.</p></p>
