<p><ul><li>Through Hole Technology</li><li>Surface Mount Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hot Carrier Diode market is segmented into Through Hole Technology (THT) and Surface Mount Technology (SMT). Through Hole Technology involves inserting diodes into pre-drilled holes on a circuit board, offering robust performance and ease of handling, making it ideal for high-power applications. Conversely, Surface Mount Technology allows diodes to be mounted directly onto the surface of printed circuit boards, enabling miniaturization and higher assembly speeds. Both technologies cater to different design requirements and industry applications, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
