<p><p>Nasal cannulae are medical devices used to deliver supplemental oxygen to patients with respiratory conditions, promoting efficient oxygen therapy through the nostrils. This device consists of two prongs that fit into the nostrils and a long tube that connects to an oxygen supply. Its popularity stems from its comfort, ease of use, and versatility in various clinical settings, including hospitals, home care, and emergency services.</p><p>The Nasal Cannulae Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.7% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by an increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases, such as chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) and pneumonia, and the rising geriatric population requiring oxygen therapy. Technological advancements in cannula design, enhancing comfort and efficacy, are also contributing to market expansion. Furthermore, the shift towards home healthcare solutions and telemedicine has boosted demand for affordable and user-friendly oxygen delivery systems. There is a growing focus on innovative materials and the development of high-flow nasal cannulae that provide better oxygenation, aligning with trends towards personalized medicine and improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
