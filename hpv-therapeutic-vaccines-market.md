<p><p>The HPV therapeutic vaccines market includes bivalent and quadrivalent vaccines, which target different strains of the human papillomavirus. Bivalent vaccines primarily protect against HPV types 16 and 18, which are responsible for the majority of cervical cancer cases. In contrast, quadrivalent vaccines target four HPV types: 6, 11, 16, and 18, providing broader protection that includes strains associated with genital warts. Both types aim to reduce the incidence of HPV-related diseases, thus enhancing public health outcomes.</p></p>
