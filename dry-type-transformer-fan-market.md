<p><p>The dry-type transformer fan market includes two primary types: top blowing and side blowing. Top blowing fans are designed to expel air directly upwards, enhancing cooling efficiency in compact spaces, while side blowing fans direct airflow horizontally, making them suitable for larger installations with limited vertical space. Both types aim to optimize the cooling of dry-type transformers, ensuring effective heat dissipation and preventing overheating, which is crucial for maintaining operational efficiency and extending equipment lifespan.</p></p>
