<p><p>Wave power generation harnesses the energy from ocean waves to produce electricity, and it can be categorized into three main market types: mechanical, pneumatic, and hydraulic. </p><p>Mechanical systems utilize moving parts to directly convert wave motion into mechanical energy. Pneumatic systems capture compressed air generated by wave motion to drive turbines. Hydraulic systems use the pressure from wave-induced water movement to operate hydraulic turbines. Each type offers unique advantages and potential applications in renewable energy production, contributing to sustainable energy solutions.</p></p>
