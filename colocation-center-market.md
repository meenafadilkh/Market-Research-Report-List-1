<p><p>Colocation centers are facilities that provide space for businesses to house their servers and networking equipment. In the retail colocation market, smaller businesses rent individual racks or cabinets within a shared facility, benefiting from flexibility and scalability. Conversely, the wholesale colocation market caters to larger enterprises needing extensive space, often leasing entire rooms or data halls at a reduced per-square-foot cost. Both models offer secure environments, power redundancy, and connectivity options, tailored to different organizational needs.</p></p>
