<p><p>The Unattended Ground Sensor (UGS) market encompasses various types designed for surveillance and detection purposes. Seismic UGS detect ground vibrations from movement, useful for intrusion detection. Acoustic UGS capture sound waves to identify activity in the vicinity. Magnetic UGS sense changes in magnetic fields, ideal for detecting metallic objects. Infrared UGS monitor heat signatures, capable of identifying living beings or vehicles. "Others" include advanced sensor technologies that combine multiple detection methods for enhanced surveillance capabilities.</p></p>
