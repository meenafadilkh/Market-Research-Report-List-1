<p><p>The Dissolved Acetylene Gas market primarily comprises two types: Hydrocarbon Pyrolysis Type and Calcium Carbide Type. The Hydrocarbon Pyrolysis Type involves the thermal decomposition of hydrocarbons to produce acetylene, often resulting in a purer product ideal for high-demand industrial applications. Conversely, the Calcium Carbide Type generates acetylene through the reaction of calcium carbide with water. This method is generally more cost-effective and simpler to implement, catering to various applications, including welding and cutting in the industrial sector.</p></p>
