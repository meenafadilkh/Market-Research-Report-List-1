<p><ul><li>Classic Bluetooth Chip</li><li>BLE Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth speaker chip market is primarily divided into two types: Classic Bluetooth chips and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) chips. Classic Bluetooth chips are designed for continuous data streaming and are commonly used in high-fidelity audio applications, ensuring stable connections for high-quality sound. In contrast, BLE chips are optimized for low power consumption and are ideal for applications that require intermittent data transfer, such as smart speakers or portable devices, enhancing battery life while maintaining connectivity for basic audio streaming.</p></p>
