<p><p>The fertilizer catalysts market comprises two primary types: base metal catalysts and precious metal catalysts. Base metal catalysts, such as nickel and iron, are widely used due to their cost-effectiveness and catalytic efficiency in processes like ammonia synthesis. In contrast, precious metal catalysts, including platinum and palladium, offer higher activity and selectivity, often used in specialized applications requiring superior performance. Both types are crucial in optimizing fertilizer production, enhancing yield efficiency, and minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
