<p><p>The urostomy products market primarily consists of two types: one-piece bags and two-piece bags. One-piece bags are integrated systems where the flange and pouch form a single unit, offering simplicity and ease of use. They are often preferred for their discreetness and comfort. Two-piece bags, on the other hand, feature separate flanges and pouches, allowing for more flexibility in changing the pouch without removing the flange. This option provides convenience for users who may require frequent pouch changes while maintaining skin health.</p></p>
