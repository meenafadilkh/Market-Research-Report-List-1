<p><p>The changeover valves market primarily consists of three types: electric, manual, and pneumatic changeover valves. Electric changeover valves utilize electrical signals for automatic operation, offering high precision and remote control capabilities. Manual changeover valves require physical intervention for control, providing simplicity and reliability in low-tech applications. Pneumatic changeover valves operate using compressed air, enabling rapid, automated switching for various industrial processes. Each type caters to specific operational needs and applications within diverse sectors, including manufacturing, automotive, and energy.</p></p>
