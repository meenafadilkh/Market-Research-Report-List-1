<p><ul><li>Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN)</li><li>Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN)</li><li>Satellite (GNSS)</li><li>Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN)</li><li>Cellular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial wireless technologies in factory automation include several market types: </p><p>1. **Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN)** offers high-speed connectivity for devices within a factory, enabling seamless data transfer.</p><p>2. **Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN)** supports short-range communications, ideal for connecting personal devices or sensors.</p><p>3. **Satellite (GNSS)** provides global positioning and tracking, enhancing logistics and asset management.</p><p>4. **Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN)** caters to low-bandwidth IoT devices, ensuring long-range connections with minimal energy use.</p><p>5. **Cellular** networks facilitate mobile device connectivity and remote monitoring, enhancing operational flexibility and control.</p></p>
