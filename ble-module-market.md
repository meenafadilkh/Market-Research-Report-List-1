<p><p>The BLE module market is primarily categorized into two types: single-mode and dual-mode BLE modules. Single-mode BLE modules exclusively support Bluetooth Low Energy, ideal for applications requiring low power consumption and streamlined connectivity. In contrast, dual-mode BLE modules facilitate both Bluetooth Classic and Bluetooth Low Energy, offering versatility for a broader range of devices. This duality allows manufacturers to target various consumer needs, from energy-efficient wearables to robust audio streaming products, enhancing market adaptability and innovation.</p></p>
