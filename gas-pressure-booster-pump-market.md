<p><strong>The Gas Pressure Booster Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multiple Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas pressure booster pump market can be categorized into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed to compress gas in one operation, making them suitable for applications requiring moderate pressure increases. In contrast, multi-stage pumps utilize multiple impellers to achieve higher pressure boosts, making them ideal for demanding applications that require significant pressure enhancements over longer distances. Both types are essential in various industries, including oil and gas, chemical processing, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
