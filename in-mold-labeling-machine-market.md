<p><p>The In-mold Labeling (IML) Machine market is categorized into three types: Injection Molding, Blow Molding, and Thermoforming. Injection Molding IML integrates labels into rigid plastic products during the molding process, ensuring durability and aesthetic appeal. Blow Molding IML applies labels to hollow objects, like bottles, effectively enhancing design and branding. Thermoforming IML is used for creating various shapes from plastic sheets, allowing for versatile labeling on containers and trays. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, improving product customization and efficiency.</p></p>
