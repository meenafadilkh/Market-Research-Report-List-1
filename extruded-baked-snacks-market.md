<p><strong>The Extruded Baked Snacks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Extrusion</li><li>Cold Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Extruded baked snacks are categorized into hot and cold extrusion methods. Hot extrusion involves cooking ingredients under high temperature and pressure, producing snacks with a crispy texture, commonly used for items like puffs and chips. In contrast, cold extrusion operates at lower temperatures without cooking, allowing for the formation of denser, often nutrient-rich snacks like protein bars. Both methods offer unique textures and flavors, catering to diverse consumer preferences in the growing snacks market.</p></p>
