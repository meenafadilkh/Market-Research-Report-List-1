<p><p>The specialty geomembranes market encompasses various types, primarily High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE). HDPE geomembranes are known for their strength, durability, and resistance to chemical permeation, making them ideal for applications like landfill liners and mining operations. In contrast, LDPE geomembranes offer greater flexibility and ease of installation, which makes them suitable for applications like pond liners and agricultural uses. Both types serve critical roles in environmental protection and resource management across diverse industries.</p></p>
