<p><p>Point of Sale (POS) systems for small businesses categorize into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based POS systems operate over the internet, offering flexibility and remote access to sales data, making them ideal for businesses with multiple locations or those seeking easy scalability. On-premise POS systems, on the other hand, are installed locally on hardware, providing enhanced control and potentially lower long-term costs, but require more upkeep and are less flexible for remote management.</p></p>
