<p><p>The robotic tactile sensing system market is divided into two main types: non-integrated and integrated systems. Non-integrated types consist of separate sensors that provide tactile feedback to robotic systems, often requiring additional components for functionality. In contrast, integrated types combine tactile sensors directly into the robotic structure, enhancing compactness and ease of use. Integrated systems typically offer improved performance and efficiency as they streamline the sensing process, making them increasingly popular in advanced robotic applications.</p></p>
