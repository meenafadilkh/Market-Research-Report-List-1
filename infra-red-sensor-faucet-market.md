<p><p>Infra-red sensor faucets are categorized mainly into two market types: Alternating Current (AC) sensor faucets and Direct Current (DC) sensor faucets. AC sensor faucets operate using standard electrical outlets, providing constant power, typically featuring robust designs suitable for high-traffic areas. In contrast, DC sensor faucets run on batteries or low-voltage DC power, offering energy efficiency and easier installation in locations without electrical access. Both types enhance hygiene and water conservation, with varying applications based on power source and installation convenience.</p></p>
