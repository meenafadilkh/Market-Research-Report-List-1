<p><p>The nicotine patch market is primarily categorized into 16-hour and 24-hour patches. Sixteen-hour patches are designed for daytime use, providing a controlled release of nicotine to manage cravings during waking hours. They offer flexibility for users who may prefer to avoid nicotine at night. In contrast, 24-hour patches deliver a continuous dosage of nicotine throughout the day and night, ensuring consistent support for those aiming to quit smoking. This variety allows users to choose based on their individual needs and lifestyle preferences.</p></p>
