<p><p>The automotive resistors market comprises several key types, including shunt resistors and voltage limiting resistors. Shunt resistors are used for measuring current by providing a low-resistance path, enabling accurate current sensing in various applications. Voltage limiting resistors help to protect sensitive electronic components by ensuring voltage levels remain within safe limits, preventing damage from voltage spikes. Additionally, the "other" category includes specialty resistors, such as power resistors and pull-up resistors, catering to diverse automotive electronic needs.</p></p>
